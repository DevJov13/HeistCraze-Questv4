Data Policy for HeistCraze Quest

Introduction:

Welcome to HeistCraze Quest. This Data Privacy Policy outlines the rules and practices governing the collection, utilization, and protection of user data within our application.

Data Collection:

We gather data to enhance your experience. The app requests the following permissions:

Coarse Location: Enhances user experience through location-based features.

Post Notifications: Enables communication on updates and promotions.

AD_ID: Facilitates personalized advertising.

Network State and Internet: Ensures smooth app functionality.

Data Sharing and Use:

Third-Party Sharing: We prioritize user privacy and refrain from sharing, selling, or distributing user information with third-party apps. External services we use adhere to strict security and privacy standards.

Privacy Assurance: Your privacy is paramount. While robust security measures are in place, you can uninstall the app if you ever have data safety concerns, maintaining control over your personal information.

Data Security:

To protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction, we employ industry-standard security measures. Your data is encrypted during both transit and storage.

Data Retention:

User data is stored as long as necessary for app services. After 90 days of inactivity, your data is automatically deleted.

User Choices:

You have the following options regarding your data:

  • Request access to your data.
  • Ask for the deletion of your data.
  • Opt-out of personalized advertising.

Children's Privacy and Policy:

Age Restrictions: HeistCraze Quest is designed for users aged 18 and above. Individuals below 17 years old are advised against accessing the app due to potential content unsuitable for younger audiences.

Data Collection for Minors: We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 17. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will promptly take appropriate action.

This app may contain content not suitable for individuals under the age of 18. Since we do not collect age information from users and cannot control who downloads the app, please play at your own risk. If you believe that you are a minor under the age of 18, we strongly advise following our cautionary guidance. You can uninstall the app, as we clearly indicate that the recommended age for this app is 18 and above.

Changes to this Privacy Policy:

This Privacy Policy undergoes periodic updates. The revised policy is promptly available within the app and becomes effective immediately. We encourage regular reviews for any policy changes.
